Momos! What is it?

Momosis a famous Cino-Indian dish made out of flour(sometimes maida) stuffed with vegetables, paneer and chicken. Dip it in a bit of chutney or mioneas and you're good to go.

Ingredients:

  • flour/maida
  • chicken(if you're not a vegan)
  • vegetables
  • onions
  • masala
  1. take a cup of flour
  2. rinse it properly
  3. crush vegetables or chicken(depends)
  4. stuff them inside momo shell
  5. boil or fry (depends on the mood)
  6. your momos is ready, yeee!
  7. dip inside chutney
  8. last but not the least, Enjoy!
