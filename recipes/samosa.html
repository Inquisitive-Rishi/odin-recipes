Samosa

Samosa! What is it?

Samosais a famous North-Indian dish. It's triangular appearance make it stands out, stuffed with potato and various Indian masala adds that punch to the taste.

Ingredients:

  • maida
  • potato
  • onion
  • typical Indian masala
  1. take a cup of maida
  2. rinse, rinse, rinse
  3. make a nice dough
  4. mesh some potatoes
  5. fry those potatoes
  6. add some indian spices(for punchy flavor)
  7. stuff potatoes
  8. fry the samosa
  9. dip with tomato sauce
  10. Enjoy!
