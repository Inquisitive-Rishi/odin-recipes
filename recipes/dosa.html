Dosa

Dosa! What is it?

Dosa is a famous South-Indian dish made out of flour stuffed with potato, paneer and masala. Dip it in a bit of chutney and you're good to go.

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • masala
  • potato
  • onion
  • paneer

steps:

  1. take a cup of flour
  2. rinse it properly
  3. mesh some potatoes
  4. mix and marinate with some masala
  5. fry on pan with some oil
  6. dip a slice in coconut chutney
  7. enjoy!
